BiteDash Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

BiteDash ("we", "us", "our", or "the app") is a food delivery platform connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery drivers in Zimbabwe. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose Collected From
Phone number Account creation and sign-in Customers, restaurant owners, drivers
Name Identify your account and orders Customers, restaurant owners, drivers
Delivery address / location Route orders to the correct address Customers
Order history Track and manage orders, calculate payouts Customers, restaurants, drivers
Vehicle type Assign appropriate deliveries Drivers
Restaurant menu and business details Display restaurant listings to customers Restaurant owners
Payment confirmation details Confirm successful payment for an order Customers, via Paynow

How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and deliver food orders
  • Connect customers with restaurants and delivery drivers
  • Calculate and track payouts owed to restaurants and drivers
  • Communicate with you about your orders

Payment Processing

BiteDash uses Paynow, a licensed Zimbabwean payment gateway, to process payments made via EcoCash, OneMoney, InnBucks, and other supported methods. BiteDash does not collect or store your mobile money PIN, banking password, or full payment card details. Payment credentials are handled directly by Paynow and the relevant mobile money provider.

Where Your Data Is Stored

BiteDash stores account and order data using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service operated by Google. Data may be processed on servers outside Zimbabwe as part of Google's global infrastructure.

Sharing of Information

We share information only as needed to operate the service:

  • Restaurants see the order details and delivery address needed to prepare and hand off an order.
  • Drivers see the pickup and delivery location needed to complete a delivery.
  • Paynow receives payment information needed to process a transaction.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain order and account data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to calculate and complete outstanding payouts to restaurants and drivers. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see below).

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us using the details below. We will respond to deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, except where retaining certain records (such as completed transaction history) is required for legitimate accounting or legal purposes.

Delete Your Account and Data

To request deletion of your BiteDash account and associated personal data, email shadreckmushonga@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request", including the phone number your account is registered under.

We will process your request as follows:

  • Your account and profile information will be deleted within 30 days of your request.
  • Your order history will be deleted, except where retaining transaction records is required for legitimate accounting, tax, or legal purposes — in which case such records are kept only as long as legally required and are not used for any other purpose.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Children's Privacy

BiteDash is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
